# Opportunity Scan Example

## Input

快速判断一个新出现的产品趋势是否值得投入正式研究。只允许使用公开资料，不使用付费数据或外部联系。

## Expected Routing

- Mode: opportunity scan
- Use: research protocol, evidence standard, source map, and only the topic modules needed
- Skip: full market sizing unless it changes the screening decision
- Output: continue, stop, or collect more evidence
- Gate: paid data, outreach, private communities, and personal data require separate approval

## Pass Condition

The agent does not turn an unverified trend signal into a market fact and does not run every research module mechanically.
