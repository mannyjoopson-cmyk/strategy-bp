# Standard Research Example

## Input

评估一个面向明确目标市场的订阅产品是否值得进入产品定义阶段。覆盖市场、用户、竞品、商业可行性和进入能力，只使用公开可核查资料。

## Expected Routing

- Mode: standard research
- Define decision, scope, hypotheses, and falsification conditions
- Use A-D evidence levels and independent-source checks
- Activate relevant market, user, competitor, and commercial modules
- Mark missing company capabilities or constraints as pending confirmation

## Pass Condition

The recommendation includes what to do, what not to do, validation metrics, scale conditions, and stop conditions without upgrading to paid diligence automatically.
